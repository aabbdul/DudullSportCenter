About Us

Dudull is a sports center that offers high quality badminton and futsal facilities and international standard courts, comfortable changing rooms, a canteen, and a large parking area. Besides providing an optimal environment for training and matches, Dudull also organizes various programs and events such as training classes and regular tournaments. With a commitment to developing sporting talents and interests in the community, Dudull is the first choice for sports lovers who are looking for a complete and comfortable place to play and develop their skills with friends and family.
